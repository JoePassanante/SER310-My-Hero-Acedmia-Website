Distinguished Students

Inasa Yoarashi

Inasa is a very tall and well built young man with buzzed, dark brown hair and black eyes.

Inasa's hero costume consists of a thick brown suit pinned together with yellow buttons. The collar is decorated with fur and his costume includes a cape that drapes over Inasa's back and almost conceals the jetpack-like propulsion system attached to his back.

Inasa's left arm is completely concealed by the thick, brown material and his hand sports a large tan glove with air pipes on it. In contrast, his right arm is completely exposed other than the skin tight blue sleeve that appears to be apart of his undersuit.

There are several tubes on Inasa's torso much like the one on his left hand that is meant to expel wind power to aid his Quirk. More tubes surround his ankles, which are left partially uncovered much like his right arm. His knees and left shoulder are decorated with plates that look similar to gas masks. Gale Force completes his costume with matching brown shoes and the Shiketsu's uniform hat decorated further with a pair of goggles.

Camie Utsushimi

The real Camie Utsushimi is a normal teenage girl who comes off as far less serious as most of her Shiketsu peers. Seiji believes she's an utter fool but their teacher describes her as simply being bubbly. She is very laid back, straightforward, and tends to be quite talkative.[1]

Camie can also be very flirtatious and Inasa believes she has great "interaction techniques".[2] Camie tends to speak using very trendy slang terms that makes her difficult to understand with some people. She also apparently likes kids

Seiji Shishikura

Seiji Shishikura is a young man with small slanted black eyes and straight purple hair that covers his left eye. In his school uniform, Seiji wears the same white collared shirt and dark pants as all Shiketsu High students. He also sports Shiketsu's signature hat whether he be in uniform or in his hero costume.

Seiji's hero costume consists of a black overcoat with red trimmings. The collar covers Seiji's entire neck and is folded over. The sleeves of the coat are cut short and ended with a thick red cuff that is strapped to the upper part of the sleeve. His left sleeve also includes a smaller white cuff. Seiji's overcoat reaches down past his knees and its torso doesn't have any designs other than red straps that appear to connect the two layers of the coat. He sports white, hard gloves that leave his index and pinky fingers exposed by connecting to the rest of his hands with red straps. Seiji completes his costume with black boots with red soles, a red mask, and Shiketsu's trademark hat.
